Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our technology wholesale and procurement services.

Do you provide bulk orders?

Yes, we specialise in bulk IT procurement and wholesale distribution. We manage logistics and supply chain management for large-scale infrastructure projects across the UK, from 10 units to 10,000+.

What products do you supply?

We supply a comprehensive range of IT hardware including business laptops, desktops, enterprise networking equipment, monitors, peripherals, and computer accessories. We also provide software licensing for businesses of all sizes.

Do you offer technical support for supplied hardware?

Absolutely. All products supplied by KVN Tech come with full manufacturer warranties. Additionally, our technical account managers are available to assist with configuration, deployment, and post-purchase support.

How fast is the delivery for wholesale orders?

Typically, in-stock items are shipped within 24 - 48 hours. For large-scale bulk orders requiring specific configurations, our team will provide a customised delivery timeline as part of your quote.

Can I request a custom quote for a specific IT project?

Yes, we encourage custom enquiries. You can use our "Get a Quote" form or contact us directly at 07344603968 to discuss your specific requirements with an IT specialist - completely free of charge.

Are your products covered by warranties?

Yes. All products are 100% genuine and come with full manufacturer warranties. We only source from authorised distributors and certified channels, so you can be confident in every purchase.

Do you serve businesses outside of Dartford?

Absolutely. While we are headquartered in Dartford, we supply IT hardware and software to businesses throughout England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland. We have logistics partners across the UK.

Still Have Questions?

Our team is happy to help. Reach out and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.
